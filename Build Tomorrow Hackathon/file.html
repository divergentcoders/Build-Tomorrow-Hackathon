Petrol Filling

Today we introduce you to the next generation technology of petrol filling and payment. We all have vehicles and are wasting our precious time on standing in a queue and slogging our energy in the frustration of the long queue of vehicles at petrol pumps We at Auto Petro Pay (APP) gives you an application in which you can set the queue for upcoming fuel fillings.

Auto Petro Pay

The application will develop a QR code. You can either paste this QR code on vehicle or show it from your phone. The person at the pump will scan that code. The amount will automatically be transmitted to the petrol dispenser machine(a chip containing Auto Petro Pay (APP) code will be placed inside the machine) and the amount will be deducted from your wallet.
